Bank Marketing Campaign Analysis


Overview

This project analyzes a bank marketing campaign to identify which customer segments are most likely to subscribe to a term deposit. Google BigQuery was used for SQL analysis, and Power BI was used to build an interactive dashboard that explores customer demographics and financial characteristics influencing campaign success.

Business Problem

A financial institution wants to improve the effectiveness of its marketing campaigns by identifying the customers most likely to subscribe to a term deposit. By understanding which customer segments have the highest conversion rates, future marketing campaigns can be better targeted, increasing campaign efficiency and return on investment.

Dataset
Source: UCI Bank Marketing Dataset
Industry: Banking / Marketing
Total Records: 4,521 Customers

The dataset contains customer demographic information, financial characteristics, and the outcome of a direct marketing campaign.

Key Insights
Conversion rates vary across different occupations.
Customer age groups respond differently to marketing campaigns.
Customers with and without housing loans exhibit different subscription behavior.
Personal loan status also influences campaign success.
These insights can help improve future marketing targeting strategies.
Business Recommendations
Focus future campaigns on customer groups with higher historical conversion rates.
Develop specialized marketing strategies for lower-performing customer segments.
Consider customer financial characteristics when selecting marketing audiences.
Continue monitoring campaign performance to improve future campaign effectiveness.
